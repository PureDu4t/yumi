Yumi is a multimedia composer, arranger, sound designer. He is a jack of all trades, writing across a multitude of ensembles, including symphonic orchestras, orchestral hybrids, chamber groups, modern rhythm sections, world instruments and vocal groups; and styles such as world, pop, rock, jazz, and classical. Influenced by his experience living in China, Zimbabwe, Uzbekistan, and The United States, his works resemble a fusion of cultures and embrace an extensive use of ostinatos.
